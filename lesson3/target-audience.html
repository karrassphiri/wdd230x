Who

Small and medium enterprenuers and large scale business and those who are interested in starting up their own businesses.


Age range:

20 to 50


Income level

Income level will range from MK5,000 to MK 20,000,000

Motivation

Create ideas to start up small, medium and large businesses. Find simple loans at low interest rates and collaborate with business entities operating in the same Industry.

    Scenarios

    1. Senario 1 is LCCI beneficial to me and my ogranisation?
    2. Senario 2What is the idea behind LCCI?

      3. To bring together different stakeholders and business owners and promote small scale businesses and provide services that will improve the living standards of people in the city of Lilongwe.

    3. Senario 3What services will I get from LCCI?

      4. Financial aid, business ideas through trainings and information on the govenment laws about businesses and have my business or company be part of LCCI promotional campaigns.

    4. Senario 4What are the successes of LCCI?

      5. Has operated for five years with a yearly increase of membership. Has managed to help new interprenuers start businesses and manage them successfully and has supported financially those businesses that were in the liquidity stage by offering loans at low interests.

    5. Senario 5What are the future plans of LCCI?

      6. To expand and operate in all major cities of Malawi. Plans to increase their promotional campaigns and incorporate International interprenuers in their business seminars and trade fairs.
